The business


Project Overview


FitMeal is an Healthy meal delivery service that offers weekly meals plans based on customer food preferences to be delivered every week at his door. The client realised that many users preferred to choose meals freely without having to subscribe for a specific weekly plan and asked me to redesign his Home Page, Product Search, Product Detail and Checkout pages in order to provide a better service and differentiate from his competitors.


Discovery: Competitive Analysis


Project Overview


I started this project by making a competitive research that helped me to make some business choices and to spot any gap in the market this business could resolve by implementing additional features and service design.


The aim was to understand:

  • Client market; Who, Why, How potential users buy this kind of product.
  • Industry Trends and the way other business structure their eCommerce.
  • Product information provided, focusing on Product Search/Detail Pages.
  • Web Standards to be considered.
